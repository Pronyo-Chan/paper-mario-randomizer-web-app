General Tips
  • A new key item was added to the game: Homeward Shroom. You can press A in the key items menu to use it, and it allows you to warp back to the game's starting location.
  • You can now drop items on the overworld by pressing R + C-Left.
  • Berry items will now go in the Key Items inventory the first time you collect a specific berry type, so you don't need to manage your inventory for their progression usage. For theBlue Berry, the first two items will go into Key Items, as it is also needed for the well.
  • If partners are shuffled, completing the "sidequest" that should unlock them grants an item. Although, if you started with a partner already unlocked, their sidequest will already be done. In Bow's case, that means the Boo's Mansion shop and Gusty Gulch gate will be opened from the start.
  • At Shooting Star Summit, Merluvlee can be paid to obtain item location hints. The key items hint can only be essential items, while badges are a short filtered list of what most consider to be the "best" badges.
  • Many bushes will contain items or coins when pressing A on them, trees will drop items when hammering them.
 Easily Missable Locations
  • Pleasant Path: Right outside of Koopa Village, there are 3 brown blocks. Hitting them in the order "Left, Right, Up" will spawn an item block.
  • Dry Dry Desert: In the screen directly north of Kolorado's camp, there is an empty yellow block on the ground. There are 3 item blocks that spawn by hitting it with Mario's hammer. The number of total required hits has been reduced from 100 to 10.
  • Gusty Gulch: There is a small house with a coin block hidden inside.
  • Lavalava Island: On the beach west of the village, the rightmost tree gives a second item if you re-enter the map and smash it again (Originally Star Piece and Coconut items).
  • Yoshi Village: There is a Yellow Yoshi that will give you an item if you give him a Tayce T. recipe item.
  • Flower Fields: Some vines will drop an item if you touch them on the wall.
  • Flower Fields (East): There are 3 trees on the path to Petunia. Hitting them in the order "2,3,1" will drop an item.
  • Flower Fields (East): You can throw a Blue Berry in the well and get an item in return
  • Shiver City: Resting at the Toad house gives an item. Resting there after putting the Scarf and Bucket back on the snowmen will grant an additional 5 items.
  • Shiver City: The mayor will give an item if you talk to him, but only after getting the item from Merle in Starborn Valley.
