General Tips
  • A new key item was added to the game: Homeward Shroom. You can press A in the key items menu to use it, and it allows you to warp back to the game's starting location.
  • You can now drop items on the overworld by pressing R + C-Left.
  • If partners are shuffled, completing the "quest" that should unlock them grants an item. Altough, if you started with a partner, their quest will already be done. In Bow's case, that means the Boo's Manor shop and Gusty Gulch gate will be opened from the start.
  • At Shooting Star Summit, Merluvlee can be paid to obtain item location hints. The key items hint can only be esssential items, while badges are a short filtered list of what most consider to be the "best" badges.
  • Many bushes will contain items or coins when pressing A on them, trees will drop items when hammering them.
 Easily Missable Locations
  • Pleasant Path: Right outside of Koopa Village, there are 3 brown blocks. Hitting them in the order "Left, Right, Up" will spawn an item block.
  • Dry Dry Desert: In the screen directly north of Kolorado's camp, there is an empty yellow block on the ground. There are 3 item blocks that spawn by hitting it. The number of total required hits has been reduced from 100 to 10.
  • Gusty Gulch: There is a small house with a coin block hidden inside.
  • LavaLava Island: On the beach west of the village, the rightmost tree gives a second item if you reenter and smash it again (Originally star piece and coconut).
  • Yoshi Village: There is a Yellow Yoshi that will give you an item if you give him a Tayce T. recipe item.
  • Flower Fields: Some vines will drop an item if you touch them on the wall.
  • Flower Fields (East): There are 3 trees on the path to Petunia. Hitting them in the order "2,3,1" will drop an item.
  • Shiver City: Sleeping at the Toad house gives an item. Sleeping after putting the Scarf and Bucket back on the snowmen will grant an additional 5 items.
  • Shiver City: The mayor will give an item if you talk to him, but only after getting the item from Merle in Starborn Valley.
